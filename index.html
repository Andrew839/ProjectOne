I am a student at San Francisco State University, currently majoring in visual communications design,
and minoring in theatre arts; this is my third year here, and I have one more year left.
I live in South San Francisco, which is around 15 minutes away from San Francisco,
neighboring cities like San Bruno and Daly City.

I have quite the amount of things I like to do, and honestly I had no idea I had this many hobbies until recently.
I like to play and watch sports, though as of late I have been doing more of the latter than the former.
I am into basketball in particular, and I like to watch the Golden State Warriors as they are the local NBA team,
however I do like to watch the NBA as a whole because I just like watching people play basketball,
as someone who has been in the players' position (to an extent) in the past.

Another hobby I have is playing video games, and they have been a huge part of my life.
I have been playing video games since I was a child, around the age of probably 8 or 9 years old.
I like to play a variety of games, but I am particularly fond of RPGs, due to the stories they tell.

Speaking of stories, I also read books, but as of late I haven't gotten the time because of school work.
I like to read fantasy novels, and I have read a lot of them:
for example, the Percy Jackson series, the Harry Potter series, and the Inheritance Cycle series to name a few.
But I read those all in the past, and I am not entirely sure why I stopped, and am looking to pick them up again when I can.

Some other things about me are that I also watch anime, listen to music and of course, I like food.
Because it's delicious. Most of the time. Anyways, that's besides the point.
All of these things I have listed are what have helped me become who I am,
not just as a person overall, but as a creative person who has now rediscovered that side to him,
and is now looking to fully immerse himself in that world and its various subworlds.
